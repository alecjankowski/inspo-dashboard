Internet Folklore → Prestige Film

The Backrooms went from a 2019 YouTube liminal space video to an A24 feature with Chiwetel Ejiofor. This is the clearest signal yet that internet mythology has become a legitimate IP pipeline. Watch for more creepypasta-to-screen deals.

April Fools as Brand Strategy Lab

The best April Fools campaigns this year weren't jokes — they were brand positioning experiments. Glossier x Fishwife tested category-crossing collabs. Dyson tested multi-day influencer teases. Dunkin gave away 1M real coffees disguised as a prank. The "joke" is now a strategy sandbox.

Craft as Concept

Liza Lou's beaded paintings and Tom Leighton's UV botanicals share a thread: extreme material craft used to make you see something familiar in a completely new way. Not decoration — transformation. This is where fine art and brand experience overlap.
